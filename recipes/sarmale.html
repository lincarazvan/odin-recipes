Sarmale

Description

Sarmale are a culinary dish known in several countries such as Turkey, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Serbia, etc.

The Romanian word "sarma" is taken from the Turkish language, where it means "to wrap".

Ingredients

  • Cabbage leaves
  • Rice
  • Minced meat

Steps

  1. Mix the minced meat with the rice
  2. Roll the composition from above into a cabbage leaf
  3. Boil the sarmale
