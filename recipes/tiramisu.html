Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert.

Its origin is disputed between the Italian regions of Veneto and Friuli Venezia Giulia.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Coffee
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Mascarpone
  • Cocoa

Steps

  1. Make a mixture of eggs, sugar and mascarpone
  2. Dip the ladyfingers in coffee
  3. Put them in a tray
  4. Top them with the mixture
  5. Repeat with as many layers as you like
  6. Top them with cocoa powder
  7. Cool the product in a fridge before serving
